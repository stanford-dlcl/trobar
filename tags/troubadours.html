A Troubadour's Guide to Wine Pairing

Submitted by Ashleigh Wais on Sat, 08/17/2019 - 06:16

For this project, I sought to combine my interested in wines from the Languedoc region with my passion for graphic design. After going on a number of amazing treks up mountains to visit castles and visit medieval sites, I noticed that wine always added a new element to the dining experience. Yet unlike traditional wine pairings that often only combine wine and food, I found that wine is also deeply connected to the broader context.
